# Economy & Tokenomics (EN)

- **Ledger core:** Tracks balances, IOU, and Trust Score; enforces debt limits and blocks abusive peers.
- **Micropayments:** Off-chain claims/debts for bandwidth and services; balance reconciliation on handshake.
- **Settlement:** Periodic ERC-20 payouts for accumulated credits; IOU supports barter/credit lines.
- **Staking for exits:** Exit/CDN providers can stake ZEO to raise limits and priority; misconduct reduces trust and can be slashed.
- **Pricing signals:** `VpnExitAgent` advertises `price_per_mb`; Pathfinder can pick cheapest vs fastest.
